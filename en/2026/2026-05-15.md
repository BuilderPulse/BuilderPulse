### What pricing and monetization models are worth studying?

**🔍 Signal**: Worth studying today: the $15/month Wispr Flow refusal, Voremi's 215 users and $6 MRR, a $162 MRR early SaaS, a $335-in-20-days launch, SubChecks making $1,000, Arcstory selling for $42,000, SaaSOffers at $3K MRR, and Indie Hackers examples at $65K/month, $15M+ ARR, and $37M ARR.

**Takeaway**: Price the saved consequence, not the code: cost reports, compliance checks, and distribution loops have clearer value than generic AI access.
